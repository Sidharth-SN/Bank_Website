About us

FAKE BANK

FAKE Bank, with nearly 70,000 employees and $54 billion in assets as of December 31, 2021, is the parent company of Fakes Bank National Association, the fifth-largest commercial bank in the India. The Minneapolis-based bank blends its relationship teams, branches and ATM network with digital tools that allow customers to bank when, where and how they prefer.
